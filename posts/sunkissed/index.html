Sunkissed, from the Vandalism #72 soundtrack

April 17, 2022 One-minute read
music • sid • c64

We made an exclusive background tune for the 72nd issue of the Commodore 64 disk magazine Vandalism News. While you are reading the magazine, ours is one of the tunes you can select.

The other tunes in the jukebox are by Laxity, Shogoon, Mutetus, Stinsen, Magnar, Fegolhuzz and Qdor, which is an impressive line-up to be part of!
