The Hangover (after last night with Stinsen)

March 31, 2021 One-minute read
c64 • music

It has been about 30 years since Yavin and I collaborated on a Commodore 64 tune, so to celebrate, here is a new tune!

This is our entry to the Last Night melody compo on CSDB, a competition organised by Stinsen. The challenge was to come up with a melody for a short little tune that Stinsen made. A lot of freedom is permitted, as long as the original chord progression is recognizable.

This was the starting point:

Last night, by Stinsen (mp3)

And this is our version:

The Hangover (after last night with Stinsen), by Yavin, Youth and Stinsen (mp3)

We got very positive reactions on CSDB: The Hangover!
